USD1 of LUCK ($WLL) Lottery 機制說明

機制介紹

每次買賣將收取 5% 稅金：

  • 4% 用於獎池（USDT）
  • 1% 用於營銷基金

當區塊達到指定倒數後，系統自動開獎，選出一位中獎者。

錢包若要成為候選人，需持有足夠 $WLL 且未被排除。

中獎者將獲得整個 USDT 獎池。

Mechanism Introduction

Each buy/sell transaction incurs a 5% tax:

  • 4% goes to the USDT prize pool
  • 1% goes to the marketing fund

When the countdown block reaches zero, the system draws one winner automatically.

To qualify, your wallet must hold sufficient $WLL and not be excluded.

The winner receives the entire USDT prize pool.
